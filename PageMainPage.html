Abigail Lynch ~ Costume Designer, Tailor, Stylist

Abigail Lynch is a current student at Elon University, majoring in Theatrical Design and minoring in French and Communications. While they have not yet had a chance to show off their skills on the live stage of McCrary Theater, they have worked as a costume designer and art director in a number of short films produced by the student-run production company Cinelon. Abigail is an avid artist and is devoted to putting their all into their work and being the best crewmember as possible, both behind the camera and the curtains.
